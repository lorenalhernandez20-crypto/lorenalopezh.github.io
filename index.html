My hobbies

I'm a person who finds joy in a variety of simple things, and many of them revolve around spending time with the people I love.


My absolute favourite thing to do is spend time with my children. We share a lot of similar interests, like:

  • our love for animals,
  • walks around the local lake o places with beautiful landscapes views,
  • and watching TV as the Mexican show, "El Chavo del 8".


Of course, we have our differences too!

My daughter isn't a fan of rock music, and my son doesn't really like K-pop.

My daughter prefers to read mangas, while my son is into scientific and philosophical books yet I, on the other hand, enjoy titles like The Perfume (by Patrick Süskind) and The Scorpions (by Walter Dean Myers).

Here are some other activities that make me happy:

  • Taking walks to reconnect with nature ☻
  • Listening to music. The genre I choose depends on my mood, or the activity I'm doing ☻
  • Taking photographs of things that I find beautiful or unique ☻


I also love to get lost in a good movie or book. There’s nothing quite like a film that makes me laugh until my stomach hurts or one that's so gripping I can't look away. Similarly, I enjoy reading a book with a narrative that completely captures my attention. The stories can be so different, but the feeling of being totally absorbed is always a great one.
